Who We Are

Turning Waste into Wealth Since 2069 B.S.

Trinetra Plastic Udhyog is a pioneering plastic recycling and matte manufacturing company based in Tilottama-06, Gorkotta, Rupandehi. We collect and process waste plastic materials from across Nepal, converting them into high-quality recycled plastic granules sold to manufacturers.

What makes us unique is our complete circular model — our recycled granules serve as raw materials for our in-house production of Plastic Gundri (Mattes), creating a truly eco-friendly full-cycle operation.

Reducing plastic pollution across Nepal through responsible recycling.
